a brief introduction in creative commons
the license helps creators retain copyright while allowing others to copy, distribute, and make some uses of their work â€” at least non-commercially.

1. CC BY
the downloader can do everything, as long as they credit you for the original creation.

2. CC BY-SA
like above but the downloader must share the new output based on your creation by using exactly this license.

2. CC BY-ND
the downloader can share only your original creation and has to credit you of course.

4. CC BY-NC
as long as it is for non-commercial purpose everything is legal if you get mentioned as creator.

5. CC BY-NC-SA
like above but the downloader must use exactly this license for sharing, means under identical terms.

6. CC BY-NC-ND
sharing only for non-profit use and only in the original form.

7. All Rights Reserved
only for your private unsharing experiments. it's standard strict copyright and not cc.

visit the creative commons project for an complete dive into the rules.
